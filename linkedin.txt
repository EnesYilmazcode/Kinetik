Won 1st place in the Nano Banana track at the Google DeepMind x GLITCH Hackathon at UCLA.

I built Kinetik, which is a Text-to-Animation engine. How it works is the user types a scene description and it generates a 3D animated character and environment.

I incorporated the newly released NVIDIA Kimodo model for motion generation as well as Nanobanana and Trellis from Fal.ai for 3D asset creation.

Huge thanks to Google DeepMind and GLITCH for putting this together at UCLA!
